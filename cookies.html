Cookie Policy

Last updated: 01 January 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you use it. Cookies do not contain personally identifiable information unless you have provided it to us.

Our use of cookies complies with the Norwegian Electronic Communications Act (ekomloven) and EU Cookie Directive 2009/136/EC.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies:

TypePurposeDuration
EssentialRequired for the website to function (language preference, cookie consent)Session / 1 year
AnalyticsUnderstand how visitors use the site (page views, time on site)Up to 2 years
FunctionalRemember preferences to enhance your experienceUp to 1 year

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services such as analytics providers that set their own cookies. These are subject to the privacy policies of those providers. We do not sell or share cookie data with advertisers.
