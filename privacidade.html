Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01 January 2026

1. Data Controller

SALA — Safe-Load Analytics is operated by Paulo Fernando de Barros, based in Larvik, Norway. For all data protection enquiries, please contact paulo@barros.no.

Processing of personal data is conducted in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Norwegian Personal Data Act (Personopplysningsloven).

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data when you use the SALA platform or website:

  • Contact information (name, email address, company name)
  • Professional information (job title, sector, company size)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, interactions)
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, device type)
  • Occupational data submitted through the SALA platform (anonymized biomechanical metrics)

3. Purpose & Legal Basis

We process your data for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving the SALA platform (legitimate interest / contract)
  • Responding to demo requests and enquiries (contract / consent)
  • Ensuring compliance with Norwegian occupational safety law (legal obligation)
  • Analytics to understand platform usage (legitimate interest)
  • Marketing communications, where consent has been given (consent)

4. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above. Contact form submissions are retained for 24 months. Platform usage data is retained for 12 months. Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interests

You may also lodge a complaint with Datatilsynet (the Norwegian Data Protection Authority) at datatilsynet.no.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to ensure the website functions correctly and to understand how visitors use it. For detailed information, see our Cookie Policy.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related enquiries, please contact us at paulo@barros.no or through the contact form.
