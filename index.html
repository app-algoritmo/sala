Core Innovation

The Biomechanical Algorithm

SALA calculates the Risk Index (Ri) focused on the human element — not just the vehicle. Our proprietary algorithm integrates four critical biomechanical factors.

Lumbar L5/S1 Analysis

Real-time monitoring of vertebral compression during load lifting. The L5/S1 joint bears the highest biomechanical stress, making it the critical point for injury prevention.

Ccompress = Fload × dmoment × kposture

Surface Factor (Sf)

Dynamic adjustment for critical conditions — Norway's harsh winters with ice and snow can increase spinal stabilization effort up to 3×. The algorithm adapts risk thresholds in real time.

Sf = μice × θslope × Wseasonal

Vibration Exposure (ISO 2631-1)

Protection against whole-body vibration (WBV) to prevent neuromuscular fatigue and chronic pain. Continuous monitoring aligned with international safety standards.

WBVdaily = A(8) × √texposure

Accumulated Fatigue

Modeling based on the Norwegian work standard (37.5h/week) and environmental stressors. Tracks cumulative load across shifts to prevent chronic overexposure.

Ftotal = Σ(Fshift × Rrecovery)
